About Vera

Vera is an intelligent personal assistant that makes phone calls on your behalf—saving you time, energy, and effort. Before each call, Vera quickly gathers key details through short questions to ensure it’s fully informed. It then handles the conversation using natural, human-like speech and provides you with a clean, structured transcription afterward—so you stay organized without ever needing to record or replay audio.

How Vera Helps

Intelligent Preparation

Vera asks targeted questions to ensure its fully prepared before making the call, saving you from back-and-forth confusion.

Automated Calling

Provide a number and purpose—Vera handles the conversation seamlessly, adapting its tone and responses naturally.

Instant Transcriptions

After the call, receive an accurate, easy-to-read summary of everything discussed—no need to record or replay audio.

Time & Energy Saver

Vera eliminates repetitive tasks and small talk, helping you reclaim time and mental energy for whats important.
